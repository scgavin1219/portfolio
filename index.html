About Me

I am a Fullstack software engineer that is passionate about creating well designed products with intuitive and aesthetically pleasing interfaces. My frontend skills include JavaScript, React, Redux, HTML, and CSS. Backend technologies include Ruby on Rails, MongoDB, Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL.

I'm a former middle school math teacher and baseball coach I am particularly interesting in building products that help connect people and help them to learn. I recently collaborated on a MERN stack app that facilitates meaningful connections between its users. I played Division I baseball at the University of Oregon and thrive working in a team environment.

When I'm not building products you can find me hanging out with my dog, Blue. I enjoy taking him to the beach, hiking, disc golfing, and mountain biking. I also enjoy playing video games and reading sci-fi/fantasty. Let me know if you have any recommendations!
